The Demographics of comic book Superheroes and Villains

Over 23,000 comic book characters currently exist

However, how well are they represented?

The goal with Super Demographics is to analyse the demographics of comic book characters, determining how representative they are of reality. To do this, we used a dataset that pulled all the characters from both Marvel and DC wikis, who are the two major comic publishers.
