Comic themes are often about good versus bad character.

50.8% of female characters played the good role versus 33.9% for males. 53.4% of males played the bad role versus 32.1%.

Good Characters tend to have Public Identities

Good Characters would normally like their fans to remember their good deeds by revealing their identities.

Bivariate analysis to determine if the ratio of public identities varies with the ratio of good characters.

There is positive correlation between the number of characters with public identities and the number of good characters.
